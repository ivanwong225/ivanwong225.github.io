Engineering World Health Project Team

On campus I am a part of the Engineering World Health project team, which creates low-cost biomedical devices for third world countries. As a member of the software subteam, I work in a small group on the software components of our interdisciplinary projects. Most recently, we implemented a backend and web application to visualize water quality of wells in India based on data collected by sensors from the Bio and Electrical subteams.
